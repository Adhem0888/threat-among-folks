SUBJECT: Threat Assessment - Echoes

I. Appearance. Echoes are identifiable by severe burn damage across their bodies, with limbs appearing as dark as soot. A persistent odor of charred flesh often accompanies them. II. Behavioral Patterns. Echoes exhibit unstable and erratic behavior, often resembling the psychological aftermath of warfare. Indicators include: Unusually wide eyes, suggesting hypervigilance or shock. Behavioral instability, including sudden outbursts of rage, twitching, snarling, or teeth grinding. Predatory tendencies; Echoes are known to exhibit animalistic behavior and will not hesitate to pursue perceived threats. Exercise extreme caution when interacting with individuals displaying unnatural or aggressive habits.

III. Known Abilities. Echoes possess supernatural capabilities that render them highly dangerous. Observed abilities include but are not limited to: Pyromancy – Manipulation of fire for offensive purposes. Bone Reconfiguration – Utilizing their own skeletal structure as a weapon. Regeneration – Have torn apart their limbs for it to grow back, typical while getting rid of bullet wounds. Be wary of any individual displaying an unusual degree of confidence or claiming comprehensive knowledge of pre-cataclysmic events.
